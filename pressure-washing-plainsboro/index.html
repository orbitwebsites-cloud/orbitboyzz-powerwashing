Do you do pressure washing in Plainsboro?

Yes — Plainsboro is one of our home towns. We cover the whole township (08536), from the established subdivisions around Princeton Meadows to the newer developments off Scotts Corner and Schalks Crossing. Text a couple of photos to (609) 297-7412 and we'll get you a preliminary price the same day.

Why does the siding on my Plainsboro house turn green?

It's the shade. A lot of Plainsboro homes back onto tree lines and preserved open space, and the north-facing walls that stay damp grow green and black algae. A soft wash treats that growth at the source with a low-pressure cleaning solution — safe for vinyl and painted trim — so it comes off evenly and stays gone longer than a quick rinse.

How much does it cost to wash a driveway in Plainsboro?

Most Plainsboro driveways land in the $130–$325 range depending on size and condition. Newer developments here often have paver driveways — we clean those at a lower pressure and can re-sand the joints if needed, rather than blasting the sand out. Send a photo for an exact number.

Can you wash townhomes and HOA properties?

Yes. Plainsboro has a lot of townhome and HOA communities, and we're happy to soft-wash siding and clean shared driveways or patios. Just check whether your HOA needs approval or has a preferred schedule, and we'll work around it.
