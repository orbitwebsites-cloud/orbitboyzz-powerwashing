Common questions

What to expect.

A few things people usually ask before booking a wash.

Do I need to be home during the appointment?

Not usually — as long as we can access the driveway, exterior walls, or patio being cleaned and there's a working outdoor water spigot nearby, you're welcome to leave the work to us.

Will pressure washing damage my siding, driveway, or patio?

We match the approach to the surface — a gentler soft-wash for siding and delicate materials, and higher pressure only where it's safe, like concrete and pavers.

Where does the water come from?

Most jobs use an outdoor spigot at your home. If that's not available, let us know when you request a quote so we can plan ahead.

How is the price determined?

Surface size, condition, access, and water availability all factor in. You'll get a preliminary range right away, and we confirm an exact price before any work begins.
