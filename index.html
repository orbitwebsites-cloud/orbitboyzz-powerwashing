Common questions

Good to know before you book.

Straight answers to what folks in Plainsboro, West Windsor and Ewing ask most.

How much does pressure washing cost in Plainsboro, West Windsor or Ewing?

It depends on the surface and its size, but here are honest starting ranges for our area: driveways run about $130–$325, house soft-washing $250–$775, patios and decks $110–$425, fences from $150, and trash-bin cleaning from $25 a bin. Text a couple of photos and you'll get a preliminary range the same day, then an exact price once we confirm the details. Right now our first 10 jobs also get founding-customer pricing.

Is soft washing safe for vinyl siding?

Yes — soft washing is the right way to clean vinyl, aluminum and painted trim. Instead of blasting the surface with high pressure, we apply a low-pressure cleaning solution that kills the algae, mildew and mold at the source, then gently rinse. It won't force water behind the siding or leave streaks, and because we treat the growth rather than just spraying it off, your house stays cleaner longer.

Do you clean trash bins?

We do. Garbage and recycling bins get washed out, sanitized and deodorized so they stop smelling and drawing bugs. It's an easy add-on to any driveway or house wash — from $25 per bin — and one of the simplest ways to freshen up the outside of your home.

Do I need to be home?

Usually not. As long as we can reach the driveway, siding, patio or fence being cleaned and there's an outdoor spigot we can use, you're welcome to leave it to us. We'll text before we arrive and again when we're done. If anything about access is tricky, just mention it when you request your quote.

Will pressure washing damage my siding, driveway or patio?

No — because we match the method to the surface. Siding, wood, composite and vinyl fences get a gentle soft wash. Concrete and pavers can take a proper surface-cleaner pass. We never use more pressure than a surface can handle, so you get an even, thorough clean without etching, splintering or wand stripes.

How does pricing work?

Surface size, condition, access and water availability all factor in. Text us a few photos and you'll get a preliminary range right away. We confirm the exact price before any work starts — no surprises — and you pay when the job's done and you're happy with it.

Where does the water come from?

Most jobs run off the outdoor spigot at your home. If you don't have one available, let us know when you request a quote and we'll plan around it.

What areas do you serve?

We're based right here and focused on Plainsboro, West Windsor and Ewing, New Jersey. We're also booking nearby Mercer and Middlesex County towns as we grow — if you're close by, ask and we'll let you know.
