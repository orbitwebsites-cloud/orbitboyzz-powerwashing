## Brand Voice — 3 Rules

Everything Orbit PowerWash says — texts, quotes, posts, voicemail — follows these three rules.

### 1. Local, not national

Talk like a neighbor who works in Plainsboro, West Windsor, and Ewing. Name the towns. Skip the generic "serving the tri-state area" filler.

- **Good:** "I'm out in West Windsor most of this week — I can swing by and look at your driveway Thursday."
- **Bad:** "Our service professionals are available throughout the greater region to accommodate your needs."

### 2. Plain-spoken, not corporate

Short sentences. Real words. Say what you'll do, what it costs, and when. No jargon, no "solutions," no "leveraging."

- **Good:** "House wash is a soft wash — low pressure and a cleaner that kills the green stuff without hurting your siding. $XXX, done in about 2 hours."
- **Bad:** "We deploy an advanced low-pressure application methodology to optimize exterior surface restoration outcomes."

### 3. Responsive, not scripted

Reply fast and like a human. Answer the actual question. A same-day text beats a perfect paragraph sent tomorrow.

- **Good:** "Got it — thanks for the photos. That patio's very doable. I can do Saturday morning for $XXX. Want me to lock it in?"
- **Bad:** "Thank you for contacting us. Your inquiry is important to us and a representative will respond within 3–5 business days."

## Standard Service Descriptions

Reusable, verbatim, in quotes, texts, the website, and posts. One paragraph each.

**Driveway Washing.** We surface-clean your concrete or paver driveway to lift out dirt, tire marks, oil shadow, and the gray grime that builds up over the years. A flat-surface cleaner gives an even finish with no wand stripes, and we rinse the edges and walkway approach so the whole entrance looks fresh — not just the middle. Most driveways take about an hour or two and look years newer the same day.

**House Washing (soft wash).** This is a soft wash, not a blast. We use low pressure and a purpose-made cleaning solution that kills the green and black algae, mildew, and cobwebs on your siding without forcing water behind it or damaging the surface. Safe for vinyl, aluminum, and painted trim. Your house comes out evenly clean and bright, and because we treat the growth instead of just spraying it, it stays cleaner longer.

**Patio & Deck Washing.** We clean patios, walkways, pool decks, and wood or composite decks — concrete, pavers, stone, or brick — clearing the ground-in dirt, algae, and weather staining that make outdoor spaces look tired. We adjust pressure to the surface so pavers don't get chewed up and wood doesn't fur up, and we clean right up to the borders and steps. You get your outdoor space back looking ready for company.

**Fence Washing.** We soft-wash vinyl and wood fences to clear the green algae, mildew, and gray weathering that creep up the shady side and along the bottom rails. Low pressure and the right cleaner do the work — we treat the growth instead of blasting it, so wood doesn't fur up and vinyl panels don't get knocked loose at the rails. We clean both sides where we can reach and rinse the plantings along the line. Your fence goes back to looking like a fence, not a science experiment.

**Trash Bin Cleaning.** Your garbage and recycling cans get funky — we scrub them out. We wash the inside and outside, disinfect, and deodorize so the bin comes back clean and stops smelling up the garage. Bins have to be empty when we come, so right after pickup day is perfect. It's a quick add-on to any wash — most folks tack it onto a driveway or house job — and it's exactly the kind of small, gross chore people are glad to hand off.
