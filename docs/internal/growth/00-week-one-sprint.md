## The funnel math (read this first)

Work backward from 10 bookings. Two numbers drive everything:

- **~5% of knocked doors** turn into a quote request (this already accounts for people not home / not answering).
- **~50% of quotes** turn into a booking.

So **1 quote ≈ 0.5 booking**, and **1 knocked door ≈ 0.025 booking**.

| To get… | You need… | Because… |
|---|---|---|
| 10 bookings | ~20 quotes | 50% of quotes book |
| ~12 of those quotes | ~240 doors knocked | 5% of doors quote |
| The other ~8 quotes | Online + personal network | see split below |

**Where the 20 quotes come from:**

| Channel | Effort for the week | Expected quotes | Expected bookings |
|---|---|---|---|
| Door knocking | ~240 doors (6 sessions × 40) | ~12 | ~6 |
| Personal network | 20 texts + referrals | ~4 | ~2 |
| Free online (Nextdoor, FB, GBP, Marketplace, Craigslist) | daily posting | ~4 | ~2 |
| **Total** | | **~20** | **~10** |

**Rule of thumb per knock session:** 40 doors ≈ 2 quotes ≈ 1 booking. If you're under that, you're either knocking dead streets (pick dirtier ones — see `door-knocking.md`) or skipping the neighbor-special close.

Bookings are built with a buffer: some booked jobs will slip past Sunday, so aim to *book* 10–12 to *complete/collect* 10.
