# Free Channels — Setup & Weekly Cadence

Every channel here costs **$0**. All copy to paste lives in `ready-to-post.md`. Expected haul for the week: **~4 quotes, ~2 bookings** from online + network combined (door knocking carries the rest). Online is slower to pay off in week one — do it anyway, because it compounds and Google Business Profile takes days to verify.

Setup order for Sunday: **Google Business Profile first** (verification lag) → personal-network texts (fastest bookings) → Nextdoor → Facebook groups + Marketplace → Craigslist → WhatsApp/HOA lists.

---

## 1. Google Business Profile (do this FIRST)

Verification can take a few days, so start Sunday morning. Even unverified, the setup work is done.

**Setup checklist:**
- [ ] Go to google.com/business, sign in with a business Google account (make one if needed).
- [ ] Business name: **Orbit PowerWash** (exactly — no extra words).
- [ ] Category: **Pressure Washing Service**. Add secondary: **Cleaning Service**.
- [ ] Service-area business (you go to customers) — **do NOT list a home address as a storefront**. Set service areas: **Plainsboro, NJ** and **West Windsor, NJ** (add nearby zips if offered).
- [ ] Phone: **(609) 297-7412**. Website: **orbitpowerwash.com**. Email for contact: **quote@orbitpowerwash.com**.
- [ ] Hours: set realistic ones (e.g., Mon–Sun 8am–8pm — you answer fast).
- [ ] Services list: **Driveway Washing, House Washing (Soft Wash), Patio Washing**. Add a 1-line description to each.
- [ ] Business description: use the Nextdoor intro tone from `ready-to-post.md` — local, new, soft-wash-careful.
- [ ] Request verification (postcard/phone/video — pick fastest offered).
- [ ] Turn on **messaging** so leads can text you through the profile.

**Photos:** you have none yet on Sunday. As soon as you finish your first job (Tue/Wed), add **before/after photos** — profiles with photos get dramatically more calls. Add a couple every day you work.

**Reviews:** the moment a job is done, send the review-ask text (`ready-to-post.md`). Even 3–5 real reviews this week make the profile convert. First review is the hardest — ask your very first customer directly.

**Week-one expectation:** may not be verified/ranking in time to drive calls this week, but it's the foundation and reviews collected now pay off immediately after. Treat it as setup + review collection.

## 2. Nextdoor (highest-value online channel here)

Nextdoor is where Plainsboro/West Windsor neighbors ask for exactly this. Recommendations here convert.

**Setup:**
- [ ] Create a personal account, verify your address, join your neighborhood + nearby Plainsboro/West Windsor areas.
- [ ] Also create a **free Nextdoor Business Page** for Orbit PowerWash (same info as GBP).

**Actions this week:**
- [ ] **Sunday:** post the Nextdoor intro (`ready-to-post.md`) as a neighbor introducing a new local service. Neighborly tone, not an ad.
- [ ] **Recommendation asks:** after each happy job, ask that customer to **"recommend Orbit PowerWash"** on Nextdoor. Nextdoor surfaces businesses their neighbors recommend — this is the growth loop. Target 3+ recommendations by Sunday.
- [ ] **Reply to "does anyone know a…" posts:** search Nextdoor daily for "power wash / pressure wash / driveway / mildew / algae." Reply helpfully and offer a free quote. These are red-hot leads.

**Week-one expectation:** 2–4 leads, 1–2 bookings if you catch a "looking for a recommendation" post. The recommendations you gather now snowball next week.

## 3. Facebook Groups + Marketplace

**Groups — join 3–5 of these (search these names):**
- "Plainsboro NJ" / "Plainsboro Residents"
- "West Windsor NJ" / "West Windsor Residents / Community"
- "Plainsboro / West Windsor Buy Nothing / Buy Sell Trade"
- Local moms / neighborhood / township groups for 08536 (Plainsboro) and 08550 (West Windsor)

**Rules:** read each group's rules before posting. Many restrict promo to a specific day (often "Small Business Sunday/Monday") or a dedicated thread. Follow them — getting banned kills the channel. When in doubt, post as a helpful neighbor, not an ad.

**Actions:**
- [ ] **Sunday/Monday:** post the launch post (`ready-to-post.md`) in groups that allow it.
- [ ] **Wed–Fri:** post the before/after post (`ready-to-post.md`) once you have photos. Real results get shares and comments.
- [ ] Reply to any "who does pressure washing?" thread with a friendly offer + your number.

**Marketplace:**
- [ ] Post the listing (`ready-to-post.md`) under Services. Set location to Plainsboro/West Windsor.
- [ ] **Renew/bump it every 2–3 days** (Fri especially) so it stays near the top.

**Week-one expectation:** 1–3 leads. Marketplace and groups are hit-or-miss week one but free and fast to post.

## 5. Community WhatsApp / HOA email lists

These are trusted, closed channels — very high conversion if you have access.

- [ ] List every WhatsApp group / HOA email list / school-parent list / temple-church-community list you or family/friends belong to in Plainsboro/West Windsor.
- [ ] Post a short, neighborly version of the personal-network text (`ready-to-post.md`) — or better, ask the group admin / a friend in the group to share it for you (a referral from a member beats a cold post).
- [ ] Don't spam multiple groups with identical text back-to-back; space them and keep it personal.

**Week-one expectation:** 1–3 warm leads if you have access to even one active list. These convert well because trust is already there.

## 6. Personal network — "20 texts to people you know"

**Your fastest bookings this week come from here.** People who know you will hire you or refer you today.

**Build the list of 20** — pull from your phone contacts, and prioritize anyone who:
- Owns a home in or near Plainsboro/West Windsor,
- Is active in local groups / knows lots of neighbors,
- Would happily refer you even if they don't need it themselves.

Include family, old coworkers, gym/church/school friends, neighbors, and anyone who's mentioned house projects.

**Send the personal-network text from `ready-to-post.md` — one at a time, personalized with their name.** Don't blast a group text; individual messages get replies.

**Ask every one for two things:** (1) do they need it, (2) will they share/forward to one neighbor. A referral from a friend is nearly a booked job.

**Week-one expectation:** from 20 texts → ~8 replies → ~3–4 leads → **~2 bookings**, plus shares that seed the online channels.
